### Risk Rationale

- The monolithic SKILL.md and the quality-reviewer.md delegation pattern are the two findings most likely to cause real maintenance friction. SKILL.md's 7+ responsibilities mean any edit to one workflow concern puts the entire skill at risk of unintended changes. quality-reviewer.md adds an indirection layer without enough value to justify the extra file.
- The Shotgun Surgery on severity definitions across templates is a latent risk: the templates currently agree on terminology, but there is no shared source of truth, so drift is a matter of time.
- The remaining Minor findings are low-impact individually. They reflect normal trade-offs in a prompt-template architecture where self-containment is valued over DRY. None of them compound with each other in ways that would escalate their severity.
